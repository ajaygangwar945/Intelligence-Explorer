Language Revolution

Large Language Models (LLMs)

Decoding the massive neural systems that master human communication.

Large Language Models are the powerhouse of modern AI. They are trained on virtually every piece of digitized human knowledge to predict, generate, and reason through text.

The Transformer Magic

At the center of every LLM is the Transformer architecture. Its secret weapon is the Attention Mechanism, which allows the model to look at every word in a sentence simultaneously and weigh their relative importance.

Self-Attention Connecting distant concepts within a single context window.
Pre-training The massive learning phase where models absorb language rules.

GPT-4 BERT RoBERTa Llama 3 Mistral PaLM

Pre-training vs. Fine-tuning

Pre-training builds the "general knowledge" of the model, while Fine-tuning narrows that knowledge down to be excellent at a specific task—like legal advice, coding assistance, or medical diagnosis.
