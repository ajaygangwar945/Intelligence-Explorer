AI & Machine Learning Foundations

The science of teaching machines to learn from data without explicit programming.

What is AI & ML?

Artificial Intelligence (AI) is the broad science of mimicking human intelligence. Machine Learning (ML) is a sub-discipline where we build algorithms that improve automatically through experience.

Major Learning Paradigms

Supervised Learning

Learning with a teacher. We provide the machine with "labeled" data (input and correct output) until it can predict results for new inputs.

Unsupervised Learning

Learning by discovery. The machine looks at raw data and finds hidden structures or groupings (clustering) on its own.

Reinforcement Learning

Learning through trial and error. An agent acts in an environment and receives rewards or penalties based on its decisions.
