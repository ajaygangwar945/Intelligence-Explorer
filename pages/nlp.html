Natural Language Processing

The art of interpreting, transforming, and generating the complex web of human language.

NLP is a subfield of AI focused on enabling computers to understand human language in a way that is meaningful and useful across varied contexts.

The Dual Mission

NLU (Understanding): Decoding intent, sentiment, and entities from raw text inputs.

NLG (Generation): Manufacturing coherent, contextually appropriate human language outputs.

The Preprocessing Pipeline

Before an AI can "read," text must be meticulously cleaned and structured:

Tokenization Lowercasing Lemmatization Stopword Removal Vectorization Spell Correction

Sentiment Analysis

One of the most powerful NLP tasks is determining the emotion—positive, negative, or neutral—expressed in text data. This is critical for customer service, market research, and social media monitoring.

Dialogue Systems

Modern chatbots and virtual assistants use complex NLP to manage multi-turn conversations, ensuring each response is not just correct, but helpful and natural.
