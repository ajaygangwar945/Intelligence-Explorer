Prompt Engineering

The surgical art of designing instructions that unlock the full potential of AI.

Prompt engineering is the process of iteratively refining the queries and instructions we give to models like GPT to ensure exact, high-quality results.

Primary Techniques

Few-Shot Learning

Don't just ask—show. By providing 2-3 examples of the desired format inside the prompt, you dramatically increase the model's accuracy.

Chain-of-Thought

Asking the model to "think step-by-step." This technique forces the AI to reason through its logic before giving a final answer.

Priming & Roleplay

Setting the context. Telling the model to "Act as an expert data scientist" changes the tone, depth, and vocabulary of the output.

Why It Matters?

Good prompt engineering reduces hallucinations, mitigates hidden biases, and ensures that the AI's output is perfectly aligned with the user's specific goals.
